by their side

A gentle, community-rooted space with Indo-Caribbean and South Asian youth in Peel — listening to stories of love, pressure, family, and faith, and slowly learning how to name harm without losing care.

Request a workshop → Sign up quietly

This work doesn’t come from guessing what young people “should” learn. It comes from long conversations in hallways, group chats, mandirs, masjids, gurdwaras, and living rooms — where love, fear, and hope all sit in the same story.

About the project

by their side began when youth shared that they were carrying questions about dating, culture, and safety with nowhere soft to put them. This project is one way of saying, “You don’t have to hold that alone.”
